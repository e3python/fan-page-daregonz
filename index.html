my reasons:

  • I love denji
  • the story itself

I love denji

He’s brutally simple in a complicated world. While everyone else is drowning in ideologies, trauma, and secret plans, Denji wants food, comfort, and affection. That contrast makes the story sharper. His basic desires act like a spotlight exposing how messed up the world really is.

the story itself

It weaponizes unpredictability. The story kills off characters suddenly, flips power dynamics without warning, and refuses to protect the audience’s comfort. That constant instability keeps tension high and mirrors the chaos of the world.
